The Godown Legacy

Born from Fire, Served with Love

Godown Briyani isn't just a restaurant — it's a legacy. What started as a humble passion for authentic dum biryani has grown into one of Trichy's most loved dining destinations. Every grain of rice we serve tells a story of patience, precision, and love.

Our biryani is cooked the traditional way — slow-cooked over wood fire in handis sealed with dough, locking in every bit of flavour. The aromatic saffron, hand-ground masalas, and the finest basmati rice come together to create a biryani that's unmistakably Godown.

🔥 Fire-Cooked, Always

We believe great biryani can never be rushed. Our dum process takes hours of slow cooking over real fire — no shortcuts, no compromises. The result? Layers of flavour in every bite that linger in your memory long after the meal is done.

🌿 Fresh Ingredients, Zero Compromise

From farm-fresh chicken and mutton to hand-picked spices and locally sourced vegetables, we ensure that every ingredient that enters our kitchen meets our exacting standards. Quality is non-negotiable at Godown Briyani.

🍽️ More Than Just Biryani

While biryani is our crown jewel, our menu spans over 100 dishes — from sizzling tandoori and crispy starters to wok-tossed noodles, juicy shawarma, fresh seafood, and indulgent desserts. There's something for everyone at Godown Briyani.

📍 A Trichy Icon

Located in the heart of Trichy, Godown Briyani has become a gathering spot for families, friends, and food lovers. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or simply craving comfort food, we're here to serve you an unforgettable experience.

"From our kitchen to your heart — that's the Godown promise."
