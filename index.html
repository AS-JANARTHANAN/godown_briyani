Our Legacy

Cooked Over Wooden Fire,
Served With Love

Godown Briyani has been serving Trichy's most authentic seeraga samba biryani, slow-cooked over real wood fire in the kalyana tradition. Each grain of rice is infused with the natural smokiness that only wood fire can give — a technique passed down through generations of Tamil culinary heritage. Every order comes with our signature dalcha, fresh onion raita, and complimentary bread halwa.
